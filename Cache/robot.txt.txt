#Header: WHAT ARE ROBOTS?
#Content: 
Robots are programmable machines that can carry out a series of actions autonomously or semi-autonomously.

#Slide: 3
#Header: TYPES OF ROBOTS
#Content: 
1. INDUSTRIAL ROBOTS. Used in manufacturing.
2. SERVICE ROBOTS. Assist in tasks like cleaning.
3. MEDICAL ROBOTS. Aid in surgeries and patient care.

#Slide: 4
#Header: USES OF ROBOTS
#Content:
