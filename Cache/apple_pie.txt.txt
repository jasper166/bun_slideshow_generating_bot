#Header: HISTORY OF APPLE PIE
#Content: Originated in England, apple pie has become a symbol of American culture. It dates back to the 14th century, evolving over time with various recipes.

#Slide: 3
#Header: INGREDIENTS
#Content: Apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, butter, flour, salt, and water. Optional: lemon juice, vanilla, or other spices for added flavor.

#Slide: 4
#Header: STEP-BY-STEP RECIPE
#Content: Prepare crust, slice apples, mix with sugar and spices, fill crust, cover with top crust, bake at 375°F for 50 minutes.

#Slide: 5
#Header: TIPS FOR PERFECT PIE
#Content: Use tart apples, chill dough, vent top crust, brush with egg wash for golden color. Let cool before serving for best texture.

#Slide: 6
#Header: HEALTH BENEFITS
#Content: Apples provide fiber and vitamins. Moderation is key due to sugar and butter. Opt for whole grain crust for added nutrients.

#Slide: 7
#Header: SUMMARY
#Content: Apple pie is a beloved dessert with a rich history. Using quality ingredients and proper techniques ensures a delicious result.
