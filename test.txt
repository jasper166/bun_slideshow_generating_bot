Design Thinking is a creative process for solving complex problems by putting people at the center.
This method helps to understand the needs and desires of users, thereby developing more optimal solutions to the challenges they are facing.
Design Thinking is an iterative process, including steps such as Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Test.

The goals of this method are:
Focus on users: Understand the needs, emotions and behaviors of users to come up with the most suitable solution.
Encourage creativity: Create space for new ideas without being limited by traditional frameworks.
Continuous testing and improvement: Solutions are tested and adjusted based on real feedback from users.
